About Me

My name is Sylvain and I am a Data Analyts with around 10 years of experience.

I have been working accross a wide range of industries (Banking, Insurance, Software) as well as undertaking a nice variety of tasks and roles: Data Quality, Analytics, BI, Product Insights and Marketing Analytics.

This website is a place where I gather all the Python, R and GoogleSheets tricks that I have learned over the years and find very valuable. I tend to use these snippets of code very often and this website is an opportunity to make them available to all interested!

But there is more. You will find here tools I have built myself: from dealing with surveys or calculating statistical significance for an AB test. There are all sorts of interesting and readily available resources for Data Analyts and Marketers alike.

Welcome to my site and I hope you will find something valuable in here!
